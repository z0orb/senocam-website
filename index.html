About SenoCam

SenoCam is a mobile application that combines artificial intelligence technology with real-time camera capabilities to deliver an interactive visual learning experience. This app is designed for students, researchers, and anyone who wants to deepen their knowledge of the objects around them.

HOW IT WORKS

Open the app and point your camera at any object. SenoCam will automatically detect and identify the object within seconds. Access in-depth information about each scanned object through the interactive learning feature.

KEY FEATURES

  • Real-Time Detection: Advanced AI technology that recognizes objects with high accuracy directly through the camera
  • Interactive Learning: Chat directly with the system to obtain more information about each object
  • Scan History: All scan results are saved automatically, allowing you to access them anytime
  • Intuitive Interface: Clean and user-friendly design for all audiences
  • Fast Performance: Optimized to run smoothly on a wide range of Android devices

WHAT YOU CAN USE SENOCAM FOR

  • Education: Students can accelerate their learning process in a more interactive way
  • Research: Document and analyze objects for academic research purposes
  • Hobbies: Photography or collecting enthusiasts can identify and learn about their subjects
  • Outdoor Exploration: Learn about plants, animals, and natural phenomena while outdoors
  • Shopping: Identify products or similar items for easier comparison

PRIVACY & SECURITY

Your scan data is securely stored on your device. We do not share your personal information with third parties. Camera access is used solely for real-time detection and does not record video permanently.

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS

  • Android 8.0 or higher
  • At least 100MB of storage space
  • Device camera access
  • Internet connection for certain features

Start using SenoCam now and explore the world in a whole new way. This application continues to be developed with new and exciting features based on user feedback.
